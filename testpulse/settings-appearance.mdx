# Appearance & Branding

Customise the visual identity of your reports from **Settings → Appearance**.

## Report title

The report title appears in the header and footer of every exported document. Default: `TestPulse`.

Change it to your company or product name (e.g. `Acme QA Reports`).

## Theme colour

The theme colour is applied to:

- PDF header band and table headers
- HTML report header and section titles
- Report panel buttons in the TestPulse UI

Click the colour picker and select any hex colour. The preview bar shows the colour with your current report title in real time.

**Default:** `#0078d4` (Azure blue)

## Custom logo (PDF cover page)

Upload a PNG, JPEG, or SVG logo to display in the upper-right corner of the PDF cover page.

**Constraints:**

- Maximum file size: 1 MB
- Supported formats: PNG, JPEG, SVG
- The logo is stored locally in your browser preferences (not in ADO storage)

**To upload:** click **Upload logo**, select your file. The logo appears immediately in the preview.

**To remove:** click **Remove** next to the logo preview.

<Note>
  Logo and colour preferences are synced to your ADO account via Extension Data. They are applied on all browsers where you use TestPulse.
</Note>

## Alert thresholds

Thresholds control the colour of pass rate badges and the automated executive summary verdict.

| Threshold | Default | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pass rate (green) | 80% | At or above this: badge is green, verdict is Pass |
| Warning (orange) | 50% | Between warning and pass: badge is orange, verdict is Warning |
| Flaky test threshold | 30% | Flip rate at or above this: test is flagged as flaky |

All thresholds accept values between 0 and 100.
